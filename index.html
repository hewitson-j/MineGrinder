Watch your back...

Mine Grinder is a bloody 80’s style horror story. It starts in 2006 and is about a group of kids that decide during a Boy Scout trip after a scary ghost story around the campfire to go on a trip every year with the intent of scaring the shit out of each other. As the kids got older, the group got bigger, and the trips came scarier. The 2016’s trip was found on the dark web and the destination is to an abandoned mine in the ghost town of Abismo de Plata in the middle of nowhere, NV. Once the group gets to the town an old miner, the last resident of Abismo de Plata, tells the damn kids to “Go away and don’t come back.” The group does not listen. They head into the mine and shortly after they enter and some shenanigans, the entrance collapses. As the group tries to escape their awful and horrifying predicament, with the help of a walkie talkie and the old miner many unexplainable things start to happen. The group finds that they are being stalked by Grinder, a very malfunctioning piece of mining equipment. Bloody and grotesque murders follow as they traverse the mine… few make it out alive.
